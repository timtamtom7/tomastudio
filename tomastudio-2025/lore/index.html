Features

A journal that thinks with you

Lore combines voice-first journaling with on-device AI that finds meaning across your entries — all without your data ever leaving your phone.

🎤

Voice Journaling

Speak your thoughts — Lore transcribes them instantly using on-device speech recognition. No typing required. Your voice is your journal.

🧷

AI Threads

Lore's on-device AI finds recurring themes across your entries and weaves them into threads — the patterns and ideas that define your life.

💡

Daily Insights

Get gentle, private reflections on your entries. Mood patterns, recurring topics, and evolving perspectives — all processed on your device.

🔒

Truly Private

Your journal entries are processed entirely on-device using Apple's Foundation Models framework. Nothing is sent to any server. Ever.

📖

Life Timeline

Browse your entries as a living memoir — grouped by threads, moods, and moments. Watch your story unfold over weeks and months.

☁

iCloud Backup

Your journal is encrypted and backed up to your private iCloud. Switch devices without losing a single memory.

Privacy

Private by design, not by policy

Your thoughts never leave your iPhone.

Lore uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models — the same AI framework powering Apple Intelligence — to analyse and connect your entries. All processing happens locally on your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no data collection.

✓

No data collected

We don't collect personal information, journal entries, or usage data.

✓

No tracking

Lore does not track you across apps or websites.

✓

On-device AI only

All AI features run on your iPhone using Apple's Foundation Models framework.

✓

iCloud encrypted backup

Backups use your private iCloud — encrypted, and only accessible to you.
